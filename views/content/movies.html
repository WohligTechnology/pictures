The first gs film 'Pyar Kiya To Darna Kya’ (1998) proved to be one of the biggest success film of the year. The film starred Dharmendra, Salman Khan, Kajol, Arbaaz Khan and Anjala Zaveri. Having proved as a hit combination by public consensus, Salman Khan and Arbaaz Khan featured again with Rani Mukherjee in GS Worldwide Entertainment's second Hindi feature film 'Hello Brother' (2002) directed by Sohail Khan. 'Maine Dil Tujkho Diya ' (2002) was gs worldwide entertainment’s third venture film. This film introduced Sohail Khan and Sameera Reddy as actors, which also starred Kabir Bedi and Sanjay Dutt directed by Sohail Khan.

In 2005 GS Worldwide Entertainment produced 'Ek Ajnabee' with the superstar himself, Mr. Amitabh Bachchan who also starred in the movie along with Arjun Rampal and Perizaad Zorabian, directed by Apoorva Lakhia. National Award Winning Director of the movie ‘Parzania’ Rahul Dholakia joined hands with gs worldwide entertainment to direct its 5th movie ‘Lamhaa’ (2010) that starred Sanjay Dutt, Bipasha Basu & Kunal Kapoor.
