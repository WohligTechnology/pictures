about gs worldwide entertainment company

GS Entertainment, leading name in the Indian entertainment industry started business in 1995 as an event management and film production company.Having scaled the heights of success, the company has ventured into the diversified fields of television software development, weddings, ad films, music videos and celebrity management.

What sets GS apart is vision, talent, expertise and attention to detail which shows in the flawless planning and execution. Some of the biggest Bollywood shows in India and abroad have been co-ordinated, scripted and directed by GS.

In addition GS has produced five films "Pyar Kiya Toh Darna Kya" (1998), "Hello Brother" (2000), "Maine Dil Tujhko Diya" (2002), "Ek Ajnabee" (2005), "Lamhaa" (2010) and forthcoming thriller "Chowky".
